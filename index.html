Beta Download

To download, follow these instructions:

  1. Click the button below, make sure to allow download permissions.
  2. Then, find the downloaded .apk file in your files app, it will be named something like 'feathered trails v0.2.3.apk'.
  3. Tap it and click install.
  4. Once the app is installed, open it and make sure to enable location permissions.
Feathered Trails is currently only available on Android Last update: 30.3.2024

Features

Live location map

Tap trees for a chance to encounter a bird

View all your sightings

View all your species

Learn about bird species and listen to their sound

How To Play

How to play:

  • Tap on the trees on the map with a (1 in 10) chance of finding a bird! (trees have a 5 minute cooldown before you can try again)
  • Birds are local to your location.
  • See the birds you have found, as well as your walking stats by tapping the notebook in the bottom right.
  • Tap a species in the Species page to see more information on that species and listen to its sound.
  • For more info and to check for updates, visit the website by clicking the 'website' button.
