About

Feathered Trails is a Location based mobile game with the aim of encouraging walking while teaching players about the birds around them.

Bird data is fetched from iNaturalist within a radius of the players location

Beta Download

To download, follow these instructions:

  1. Click the 'download' button below, make sure to allow download permissions for your browser.
  2. Then, find the downloaded .apk file in your files app, it will be named something like 'feathered trails v0.2.5.apk'.
  3. Tap it and click install.
  4. Once the app is installed, open it and make sure to enable location permissions.
  5. Have fun!
Feathered Trails is currently only available on Android Last update: 20.4.2024

How To Play

  • Tap on the trees on the map with a (1 in 5) chance of finding a bird! (trees have a 3 minute cooldown before you can try again)
  • Birds are local to your location.
  • See the birds you have found, as well as your walking stats by tapping the notebook in the bottom right.
  • Tap a species in the Species page to see more information on that species and listen to its sound.
  • Set your walking goal and view your stats by tapping the 'Stats' button.
  • For more info and to check for updates, visit the website by clicking the 'website' button.
